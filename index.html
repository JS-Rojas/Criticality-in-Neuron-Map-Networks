04 · Mathematical core

A simple map, collective complexity.

xᵢ(t+1) = (1−ε) fᵢ(t) + ε h(t)
yᵢ(t+1) = a yᵢ(t) − b xᵢ(t) + c

Quenched heterogeneity enters through each neuron's stimulus parameter kᵢ. The global field h(t) couples the population.

  • 01

    Local chaos

    The Chialvo map reproduces excitable spiking and chaotic dynamics with two state variables.

  • 02

    Rare coherent regions

    Parameter disorder lets subsets of neurons transiently sustain coherent behavior.

  • 03

    Intermittent clusters

    The population alternates between synchronization and dispersion across an interval of ε.

  • 04

    Topological extension

    A Watts–Strogatz network combines intrinsic and structural disorder, broadening the observed regime.

Main result

Intrinsic neuronal diversity is enough to generate an extended region of critical-like chaotic dynamics.
